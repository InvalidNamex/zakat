## 1. Overview

حاسبة زكاة المال ("the App") is a simple, offline zakah (Islamic wealth tax) calculator. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the App handles and how it is used.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

**The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.**

The only data the App stores is:

| Data | Purpose | Storage |
|---|---|---|
| Gold gram amounts per purity (14K, 18K, 21K, 22K, 24K) | To restore your last-entered values on next launch | Local device only (SharedPreferences) |
| Hijri start-of-year date | To record your annual zakah cycle date | Local device only (SharedPreferences) |

- All data is stored **exclusively on your device**.
- No data is uploaded to any server or cloud service.
- No analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs are included.
- The App requires **no internet connection** and requests **no network permissions**.

---

## 3. How We Use the Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

- Pre-fill input fields when you re-open the App, saving you from re-entering values.
- Display your saved Hijri date for the current zakah cycle.

You can erase all stored data at any time by tapping the **reset button** (↺) in the App.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any data with third parties because **no data ever leaves your device**.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs that collect data (e.g., Firebase, Google Analytics, AdMob, Facebook SDK). The only packages used are open-source Flutter libraries for UI rendering and local storage.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Because no personal data is collected from any user, the App is safe for all ages.

---

## 7. Data Security

All data is stored locally using Android's SharedPreferences, which is sandboxed to the App and inaccessible to other applications.

---

## 8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored only on your device, you have full control:

- **Access / View:** Open the App at any time to see the stored values.
- **Delete:** Tap the reset button in the App, or uninstall the App to remove all associated data.
